WEIGHTLIFTING

health benefits ------ look cooler ------ get stronger

When you start weightlifting, an obvious change will you will start to get stronger. Your muscles are going to start getting used to moving heavy objects, and therefore you will be stronger as a result.

This is appealing because it will make every day tasks easier. Moving furniture, bringing in groceries, even pushing a shopping cart will all become easier.
