WEIGHTLIFTING

health benefits ------ look cooler ------ get stronger

Weightlifting has several health benefits, including both physical and mental health. Which means if you start weightlifting you can feel better physically and mentally!

A study done by Duke University found that 60% of peoplpe suffering from depression could overcome their depression by doing short weighttraining sessions only four days a week, rather then taking antidepressants. This isn't seen with all exercise, so running may not have the same effect.

Several studies have shown physcical health benefits such as decreasing blood pressure, lower bad cholesterol, and and improved blood circulation through strengthining the heart and blood vessles.
